---

## What is Azure for Students?

Azure for Students is an excellent resource that allows you to explore the power of Microsoft Azure with free cloud credits. This guide walks you through the verification process so you can get started with virtual machines, databases, AI services, and web deployment.

---

## Step 1: Navigate to the Azure for Students Portal

![Azure Homepage](/assets/images/dynamic/azure/azure-homepage.png)

Visit the official [Azure for Students](https://azure.microsoft.com/en-in/free/students) page provided by Microsoft to begin.

---

## Step 2: Sign In or Create a Microsoft Account

![Azure Sign-In Page](/assets/images/dynamic/azure/azure-sign-in.png)

To proceed, sign in with an existing Microsoft account, or create a new one. 

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Make sure to use your **official school/university email address** (e.g., ending in `.edu` or your institution's domain) to qualify for student verification.

---

## Step 3: Verify Your Student Academic Status

![Azure Verification Page](/assets/images/dynamic/azure/academic-status.png)

Microsoft requires verification to confirm you are actively enrolled. Follow the on-screen prompts, enter your student email, and complete the academic verification.

---

## Step 4: Access Your Student Dashboard

![Azure for Students Dashboard](/assets/images/dynamic/azure/azure-final-homepage.png)

Congratulations! Once your status is successfully verified, you will be redirected to the Azure for Students portal highlighting your active benefits, including your free credits and a catalog of free services.

---

## Step 5: Activate Your Azure Subscription

Navigate to the [Azure Portal](https://portal.azure.com/) and sign in with the verified account. Look for the "Activate" or "Claim" notification to apply the free credits directly to your active subscription.

---

## Step 6: Explore Azure Services

Now that your subscription is active, you can leverage a wide range of Azure tools:
- **Virtual Machines:** Host servers, test scripts, and build environments.
- **App Service:** Deploy your web applications instantly.
- **Azure Cognitive Services:** Embed pre-trained AI and computer vision APIs into your projects.

> [!TIP]
> Keep in mind that free student credits have usage limits and expiration periods. Monitor your active resources to prevent unexpected pauses.

---

## Step 7: Monitor Your Usage

You can easily track your remaining credits in the Cost Management section of the Azure Portal. Regularly monitoring your usage ensures you make the most of your resources without running out of credits mid-project.

---

## Conclusion: Start Building!

Getting access to Azure student credits is a major step forward in your cloud computing journey. Whether you are experimenting with serverless architectures, deploying AI models, or building web applications, Microsoft Azure provides a professional platform to learn and innovate. Enjoy building!
