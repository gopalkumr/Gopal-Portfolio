I am excited to share my experience as a Research and Development intern at IIT Delhi through the IHFC READY Programme. This initiative supports startups and entrepreneurs by connecting them with leading academic research institutions.

I was selected to work on an innovative machine learning project focused on crowd management using drone technology, which aligned perfectly with my interests in AI and computer vision.

---

## The Application Process

The application process is structured and transparent. Interested candidates can find detailed information on the [IHFC Careers Portal](https://www.ihfc.co.in/careers/). The selection process starts with an initial application review, followed by shortlisting, and finally a technical interview.

---

## Onboarding & Orientation

My internship commenced in July with an onboarding orientation at the Thyagraj Sports Complex, which hosted a national-level robotics hackathon. This provided an excellent environment to explore innovative projects and network with industry professionals, including startup founders and representatives from companies like Autodesk.

---

## Work Environment

Our daily workflow began with standup meetings led by our mentor, Dr. Akash Sondhi. His leadership style fostered a highly collaborative and supportive environment that encouraged innovation. Working alongside three other interns on parallel projects, we maintained a culture of knowledge-sharing and mutual support.

---

## Project Experience

Our team was tasked with developing an AI-powered solution to address a critical challenge in public safety: **Real-Time Crowd Management**.

Large public gatherings in India can pose significant stampede and security risks. Our solution involved deploying optimized machine learning models on drone feeds to monitor crowds in real-time, estimate density, identify potential safety concerns, and alert authorities proactively.

---

## Learning Opportunities

The internship provided invaluable exposure to research methodologies under the guidance of distinguished faculty members, including Dr. Akash Sondhi and Dr. Venkat. Working with real-world datasets and developing models with actual societal impact has been incredibly rewarding.

All project deliverables and presentations are documented at [VisionML](https://visionml.tech).

---

## Professional Development

The exposure to IIT Delhi's academic environment, coupled with the opportunity to interact with leading faculty and researchers, has provided immense networking opportunities and deep technical insights.
